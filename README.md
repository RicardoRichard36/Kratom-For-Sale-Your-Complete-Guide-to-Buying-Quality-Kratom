## Storing Kratom Properly After Purchase

Proper storage preserves the quality and potency of **kratom for sale** products after purchase.

### Optimal Storage Conditions

**Temperature**: Store kratom in cool, dry locations away from heat sources. Excessive heat degrades alkaloids and reduces potency.

**Light**: Keep kratom away from direct sunlight. UV light breaks down active compounds over time. Opaque containers provide better protection than clear ones.

**Moisture**: Humidity is kratom's enemy. Moisture promotes mold growth and alkaloid degradation. Use desiccant packets in storage containers for added protection.

**Air Exposure**: Minimize oxygen exposure by using airtight containers. Vacuum-sealed bags offer excellent long-term storage.

### Storage Container Options

**Best Choices:**
- Mason jars with tight-fitting lids
- Vacuum-sealed bags
- Food-grade plastic containers with airtight seals
- Original vendor packaging (if resealable and opaque)

**Avoid:**
- Paper bags (allow air and moisture penetration)
- Clear containers in light-exposed areas
- Non-airtight containers
- Locations near heat sources or appliances

### Shelf Life Expectations

Properly stored kratom maintains potency for extended periods:

- **Powder in ideal conditions**: 1-3 years
- **Capsules**: 1-2 years (gelatin degrades faster than powder)
- **Extracts**: 6 months to 2 years (varies by type)
- **Opened packages**: Use within 3-6 months for best quality

Dating packages upon receipt helps track freshness and rotate stock appropriately.
